PosTea

PosTea is a social media platform designed for users who like to indulge in interesting conversations over a variety of topics from all around the world. A user can follow a topic/user that they find interesting. They can also post on topic pages as anonymous in order to guard their privacy.


The frontend is created using the Flutter framework and the backend is done via NodeJS in JavaScript. This application is still under development.
